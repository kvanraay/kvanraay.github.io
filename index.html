Welcome

I am a second year PhD student in the Kerr Lab at the University of Washington. I use bacteriophages (viruses that infect bacteria) to study evolution, with a particular interest in how antagonistic interactions with their bacterial hosts shape bacteriophage evolutionary trajectories.

I am strongly committed to increasing diversity in STEM. Thus far I have done work volunteering in elementary schools and prisons in addition to teaching at the undergraduate level.
